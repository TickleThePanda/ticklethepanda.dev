Hi, I'm Panda!

Who am I?

That's a big question. I'm an all around queer nerd. I aspire (possibly naively!) to help make the world a better place, and to be friendly and kind to others. I sometimes (or often, if I'm honest with myself) take myself too seriously but I like to call that being earnest. I have green eyes, freckles, and long hair - sometimes a beard.

What do I like to do?

I have a few hobbies. I like making things (usually with web technologies), playing board and role playing games, travelling and trying out new things, and taking (mostly analogue) photos. I also enjoy listening to music, hiking, making pizza, and brewing coffee.

What about my work?

I've done a variety of things! Mostly building websites and web apps, leading agile projects and teams, and building developer platforms. I work at the Science and Technology Facilities Council (STFC) (part of UK Research and Inovation). Take a look at my résumé if you're interested in looking in more detail at my professional experience.

Where can you find me?

I share most of the code for my personal projects on @TickleThePanda at GitHub. If you want to chat (I hope you feel you can do), you can find me at @panda@tech.lgbt on Mastodon. Or you can use this form to contact me.
